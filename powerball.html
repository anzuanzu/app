Five AI Algorithms

Pro version "Hybrid Strategy" uses five different algorithms for comprehensive historical data analysis

1

Frequency Analysis

Statistics on number historical appearance frequency, prioritizing high-frequency numbers. Formula: Frequency = Number occurrences ÷ Total draws.

2

Correlation Analysis

Analyze co-occurrence relationships between numbers, finding number pairs that frequently appear together. Uses co-occurrence matrix to calculate correlation strength.

3

Pattern Recognition

Identifies odd-even and high-low distribution patterns. Combines numbers based on historical odd-even ratios.

4

Hot-Cold Balance

Balance hot and cold numbers, combining hot, warm, and cold numbers in a 2:2:2 ratio.

5

Trend Analysis

Analyze recent appearance trends of numbers, focusing on high-frequency numbers in recent periods to capture upward trends.
